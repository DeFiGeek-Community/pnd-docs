# Offsetting Borrow Interest with Rewards

To promote borrowing of base assets, the base asset borrow interest that borrowers bear becomes a bottleneck,

so punodwoɔ is designed to <mark style="color:red;">offset interest with rewards as much as possible</mark> by distributing more PND rewards to borrowers.

As shown in the graph at the bottom right of the pool detail page,

up to the reward kink supply utilization rate, <mark style="color:red;">100% of rewards are allocated to borrowers</mark>,

and after that, they are allocated in a crossing pattern so that the ratio for borrowers and lenders adds up to 100%.
