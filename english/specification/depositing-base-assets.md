# Depositing Base Assets

### Interest and Rewards

When users deposit base assets into a pool, deposit interest and rewards may be generated.

Deposit interest rates are displayed as Deposit APR on the pool detail page.

Rewards are in PND, and the reward rate is displayed below Deposit APR on the pool detail page.

Please check both.
